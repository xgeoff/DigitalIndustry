# Digital Industry

Digital Industry is a newly founded enterprise software consultancy led by operators with decades of platform delivery and Salesforce experience. The firm focuses on designing pragmatic, durable systems that reflect how regulated, high-traffic organizations actually operate.

## Pillars

- **Enterprise Platforms** – Architect and evolve resilient platforms with patterns proven in large-scale environments, tailored to your team, data, and governance needs.  
- **Salesforce & CRM** – Build Salesforce ecosystems that respect complex compliance and revenue goals without overcommitting to solutions that outpace your operating reality.  
- **Consulting & Strategy** – Translate operator insight into roadmaps, guardrails, and governance touchpoints that keep teams aligned while preserving clarity on trade-offs.  
- **Software Engineering** – Apply modern discipline, observability, and delivery practices in ways that honor the ecosystems you already run.

## Partnership approach

- **Discovery & Alignment** – Understand constraints, surface risks, and craft technical guardrails before writing code.  
- **Delivery with Transparency** – Operate in small, disciplined teams that iterate visibly and document how decisions were made.  
- **Scale & Evolution** – Help mature governance, observability, and platform ownership so systems remain healthy long after launch.
